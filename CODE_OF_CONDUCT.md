# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a respectful and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but accept responsibility for impact.
- Focus criticism on ideas, code, workflows, and documentation rather than people.
- Be clear about assumptions, uncertainty, and limitations.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks or sustained disruption of discussions.
- Publishing private contact details, credentials, patient data, or other sensitive information.
- Misrepresenting validation status, security posture, or clinical readiness.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may moderate discussions, close issues or pull requests, and restrict participation when needed to protect the community and project integrity.

Concerns can be raised privately with maintainers or through the OSIPI community contact route.
